SERVICES

CONSULTATION & EVALUATION

Our team has knowledge and experiences throughout the medical field; from ER equipment to OR equipment, to ICU equipment and more. We offer our expertise before we perform any work. We assess each situation differently and communicate with you every step of the way.

PREVENTATIVE MAINTENANCE & EQUIPMENT REPAIR

Our Biomedical Engineering Technologists are trained in Preventative Maintenance and Corrective Work. Preventative Maintenance can be annual device inspection and/or new device acceptance. Corrective work is fancy for 'repair'. 

In emergency and work cases where you need a device accepted or repaired, we will do what we can to get the job done as fast and as safe as possible.
